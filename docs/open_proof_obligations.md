# Open Proof Obligations

## Active

### PFP-003

Define finite prediction target, observable, and regime descriptors as bounded
protocol-row components.

Acceptance criteria:

- target, observable, and regime descriptors are finite and explicit
- descriptor labels are bounded and tied to a closed `PFP-002` protocol row
- descriptors remain selection criteria, not observed-catalog recovery
- descriptors do not claim benchmark success, prediction success,
  falsification success, physical validation, empirical adequacy, physical
  promotion, physical nature realization, or unified-field promotion
- Rust and Lean guards show `PFP-003` can close while the full Paper 15 theorem
  remains open

## Closed

- `PFP-001`: Upstream binding and claim-boundary scaffold.
- `PFP-002`: Finite prediction-and-falsification protocol record schema.
