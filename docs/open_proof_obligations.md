# Open Proof Obligations

## Active

### PFP-004

Define finite falsification threshold and rejection-condition descriptors as
bounded protocol-row components.

Acceptance criteria:

- threshold and rejection-condition descriptors are finite and explicit
- descriptor labels are bounded and tied to closed `PFP-002` and `PFP-003`
  records
- rejection conditions remain predeclared protocol rules, not falsification
  success claims
- threshold descriptors remain labels and finite criteria, not empirical
  validation, fit-only calibration, or simulation-only promotion
- Rust and Lean guards show `PFP-004` can close while the full Paper 15 theorem
  remains open
